Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Xolve ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the LawFlow mobile application. By using LawFlow, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01Who We Are

LawFlow is a legal practice management application developed and operated by Xolve. The app is designed exclusively for use by licensed advocates and law firms in India. We are the data controller for personal information collected through the app.

Contact: lawflow.india@gmail.com

02Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Identity information: Your name, phone number, and bar enrollment number provided during registration
  • Professional information: Bar council details, court city, designation, and firm details
  • Case and client data: Case numbers, CNR numbers, court details, hearing dates, client names, client phone numbers, case notes, and related documents — this is sensitive legal data
  • Payment information: Subscription plan, payment history, and Razorpay transaction records (we do not store card details — these are handled directly by Razorpay)
  • Device information: Push notification token for sending hearing reminders
  • Usage data: App activity and crash reports collected via Sentry for error monitoring

03How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the LawFlow service:

  • To authenticate your identity via OTP login (powered by MSG91)
  • To store and sync your case, client, and hearing data across devices
  • To send hearing reminders and firm notifications via push notifications
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To back up documents to your personal Google Drive (only when you explicitly connect it)
  • To monitor app crashes and errors to improve reliability (via Sentry)
  • To manage your referral rewards

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the LawFlow service.

04Third-Party Services

LawFlow uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • MSG91 — OTP delivery for authentication. Your phone number is shared with MSG91 solely for this purpose. MSG91 Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay — Payment processing for Pro subscriptions. Payment details are handled directly by Razorpay and are not stored on our servers. Razorpay Privacy Policy
  • Google Drive — Document storage, used only when you explicitly connect your Google account in settings. Documents are stored in your own Google Drive. Google Privacy Policy
  • MongoDB Atlas — Cloud database hosted by MongoDB, Inc. Your data is stored securely on Atlas servers.
  • Sentry — Crash reporting and error monitoring. Device and usage data may be captured when the app crashes. Sentry Privacy Policy

05Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens stored securely on your device
  • API access is rate-limited to prevent abuse
  • Sensitive plan and account fields are protected from self-modification

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong device passcode to protect your device.

06Client Data (Third-Party Personal Data)

LawFlow allows advocates to store personal information about their clients (third parties) including names and phone numbers. As an advocate using LawFlow, you are responsible for:

  • Ensuring you have appropriate authorisation to store your clients' data in the app
  • Complying with your professional obligations under the Bar Council rules
  • Complying with applicable Indian data protection laws including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

LawFlow is a tool for advocates. We do not access, share, or process client data for any purpose other than storing and displaying it to you within the app.

07Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data including cases, clients, hearings, and profile information is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Documents stored in your Google Drive are not deleted — those remain under your Google account's control.

08Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Correction: You can update your profile, case, and client information directly in the app
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account
  • Portability: You can export your data as PDF or CSV from within the app

To exercise any of these rights or for any data-related queries, contact us at lawflow.india@gmail.com.

09Children's Privacy

LawFlow is designed exclusively for licensed advocates and legal professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has registered an account, please contact us immediately.

10Legal Disclaimer

LawFlow is a practice management tool. It does not provide legal advice and is not a substitute for professional legal judgment. All case data, notes, and documents in the app are the sole responsibility of the advocate using the app.
